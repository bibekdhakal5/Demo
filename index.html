Frequently Asked Questions

Unfiltered, or raw, honey contains bee pollen, beeswax, and propolis which are removed during filtering. Filtered honey has a clearer, smoother consistency, while unfiltered honey is often creamier and contains more of the natural beneficial compounds from the hive.
Our bees forage in natural, pesticide-free environments. We do not add any sugars, syrups, or preservatives to our honey. It's 100% pure, straight from the hive to your table.
